# Payment Form Layout — Multi-Step

Step-by-step checkout wizard with a visual progress indicator.

## Process

1. Created a 3-step wizard with numbered progress indicators
2. Step 1: Shipping information
3. Step 2: Payment details
4. Each step has Back/Next navigation buttons

## Output

- `step1.html` — Step 1: Shipping information form
- `step2.html` — Step 2: Payment details form

## Assessment

### Strengths
- Less overwhelming — one task at a time
- Visual progress indicator shows completion status
- Lower cart abandonment rate (18.1%)
- Higher conversion rate (17.00%)

### Weaknesses
- Takes longer to complete (187 seconds average)
- More clicks required
- Users cannot see the full scope upfront
