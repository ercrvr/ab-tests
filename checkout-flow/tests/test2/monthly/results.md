# Pricing Display Format — Monthly

Standard monthly pricing display showing simple per-month prices.

## Process

1. Created a two-column pricing card layout (Starter + Pro)
2. Displayed prices as simple monthly amounts ($9/mo, $29/mo)
3. Pro plan has a highlighted border for emphasis

## Output

- `pricing.html` — Two-column pricing card layout

## Assessment

### Strengths
- Clean and straightforward
- Easy to understand — no mental math required
- Familiar pricing pattern

### Weaknesses
- No incentive to commit long-term
- Lower revenue per user potential
