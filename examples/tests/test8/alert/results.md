# Notification Sound — Alert

Generated a 3-second 440Hz sine wave tone (A note) in WAV and MP3 formats — a classic attention-grabbing notification sound.

## Process

1. Generated a 440Hz sine wave at 44.1kHz sample rate
2. Applied a short fade-in and fade-out to avoid clicks
3. Exported as both WAV (lossless) and MP3 (compressed)

## Output

- `tone.wav` — Lossless WAV, 3 seconds, 44.1kHz
- `tone.mp3` — MP3 compressed, 3 seconds

## Assessment

A classic notification tone. Immediately attention-grabbing due to the 440Hz frequency (standard musical A). Works well for error alerts or critical notifications where missing the sound is not acceptable.

### Strengths
- Universally recognized alert pitch
- Clear and unmissable

### Weaknesses
- Jarring in quiet or professional settings
- May cause annoyance with frequent use
