# Performance Report PDF — Executive

Generated a concise 1-page executive summary PDF with key metrics and trend indicators.

## Process

1. Identified the 4 most critical KPIs for executive audience
2. Laid out a single-page format with large, scannable numbers
3. Added trend arrows and brief commentary

## Output

- `report.pdf` — 1-page executive summary PDF

## Assessment

Exactly what an executive needs — the most important numbers in 30 seconds of reading. The trend indicators make it immediately clear whether the quarter was good or bad without reading any body text.

### Strengths
- Single page, scannable
- Large trend indicators
- Decision-relevant metrics only

### Weaknesses
- No supporting data or methodology
- Cannot answer "why" questions
